Shipping & Delivery

Do you offer White Glove Delivery?

Yes! For our 'Premium' and 'Artisan' plans, we offer complimentary White Glove Delivery. Our team will bring the item into your room of choice, assemble it, and remove all packaging materials.

How long does delivery take?

In-stock items typically arrive within 5-10 business days. Custom or made-to-order pieces may take 4-8 weeks depending on the complexity of the design.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within the contiguous United States. We are working on expanding to Canada and Europe in late 2026.

Product Care & Customization

Can I customize the fabric on sofas?

Absolutely. We offer over 50 fabric and leather options for most of our upholstery items. You can order free swatches to see and feel the material before purchasing.

Is the wood sustainably sourced?

Yes, sustainability is our core value. All our wooden furniture is crafted from FSC-certified timber or reclaimed wood to minimize environmental impact.

How do I clean my velvet sofa?

We recommend regular vacuuming with a soft brush attachment. For spills, blot immediately with a clean, dry cloth. Do not rub. Professional cleaning is recommended for deep stains.

Warranty & Service

What does the warranty cover?

Our standard warranty covers manufacturing defects for 1 year. Premium members get a 5-year warranty covering frames, springs, and joinery. Artisan members enjoy a lifetime warranty.

Do you offer repair services?

Yes, we offer repair services for out-of-warranty items for a fee. Please contact our support team with photos of the issue for a quote.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. The item must be in original condition. A restocking fee of 15% applies unless the item arrived damaged or defective.

How do I initiate a return?

Simply log in to your dashboard, go to "My Orders," and select the item you wish to return. Follow the prompts to generate a return shipping label.
